# Security

Do not probe, scan, scrape, brute-force, fuzz, or attempt to access any private
Big Stick, Unburied, Fightback, Listen Up, or Unburied Listening Worker system.

Do not report sensitive issues publicly.

Do not include credentials, private URLs, personal data, legal details, medical
details, family details, transcripts, recordings, case information, screenshots,
or internal records in public messages.

If you believe you have found a security or privacy issue, contact Faith through
her official private channels with a minimal, non-exploitative description.
