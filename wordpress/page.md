## What This Project Is

It is a private companion worker for controlled media, transcript, and social
listening review. The public page explains the boundary without exposing the
engine.

## Why It Matters

Sensitive listening work needs careful custody. This project keeps raw intake
private, routes review through protected workflows, and moves only approved
mirror-safe context outward if Faith chooses.

## How It Works

Controlled inputs enter private intake, move through protected review, and may
produce mirror-safe metadata for private Big Stick/Unburied review.
