# Hero Image Prompt

Filename suggestion: `unburied-listening-worker-hero.png`

Purpose: WordPress hero or public repo social visual.

Placement: Top of public project note.

Aspect ratio: 16:9.

Style direction: Carrot-guided Big Stick identity image. Warm, protective,
calm, serious, with private intake, review, and safe export represented as
three connected zones around the illustrated carrot icon.

Prompt: Create an original public-safe hero image for a protected listening
worker inside a private evidence-review system. Use the Big Stick carrot guide
image as the identity anchor: an illustrated anime-style Black woman with a
large carrot, treated as a joyful command-board mark rather than a product
mascot. Show a warm dark workspace with three symbolic zones: private intake,
protected review, and mirror-safe export. Use carrot orange, soft amber, deep
green, and muted rose accents. Convey dignity, privacy, careful custody, and
technical seriousness.

Negative prompt / avoid: No police imagery, no surveillance wall, no court
documents, no family photos, no medical or legal records, no real screenshots,
no logos, no celebrity likenesses, no copyrighted characters, no tokens, no
email addresses, no case numbers.

Alt text: Carrot-guided protected listening workflow with private intake,
protected review, and mirror-safe export zones.

Privacy notes: Safe to generate now. Does not require private screenshots.
