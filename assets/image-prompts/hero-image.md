# Hero Image Prompt

Filename suggestion: `unburied-listening-worker-hero.png`

Purpose: WordPress hero or public repo social visual.

Placement: Top of public project note.

Aspect ratio: 16:9.

Style direction: Original abstract editorial technology image. Warm, protective,
calm, serious, with private intake, review, and safe export represented as
three connected zones.

Prompt: Create an original abstract hero image for a protected listening worker
inside a private evidence-review system. Show a warm dark workspace with three
symbolic zones: private intake, protected review, and mirror-safe export. Use
soft amber, deep green, and muted rose accents. Convey dignity, privacy,
careful custody, and technical seriousness. No readable text.

Negative prompt / avoid: No police imagery, no surveillance wall, no court
documents, no family photos, no medical or legal records, no real screenshots,
no logos, no celebrity likenesses, no copyrighted characters, no tokens, no
email addresses, no case numbers.

Alt text: Abstract protected listening workflow with private intake, protected
review, and mirror-safe export zones.

Privacy notes: Safe to generate now. Does not require private screenshots.
